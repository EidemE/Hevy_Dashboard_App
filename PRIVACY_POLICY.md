# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-12

## Summary
Hevy Dashboard processes workout CSV data locally on your device to display your personal dashboard. The data is not uploaded to our servers and is not used for any other purpose.

## Data We Collect
- Workout CSV files you import from Hevy.

## How We Use Data
- To build and display your dashboard and statistics in the app.

## Data Sharing
- We do not share your data with third parties.

## Data Storage and Retention
- Data is stored locally on your device.
- You can delete the data at any time from the app.

## Security
- We do not transmit your data to external servers.

## Children
- The app is not directed to children under 13.

## Contact
If you have questions, contact the developer via the GitHub page listed in the app.
